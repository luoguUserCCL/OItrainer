1. daily

  • adj 每日的；日常的；
  • adv 每天；
  • n 日报；

    I read the daily newspaper every morning.
    我每天早上阅读日报.

dailies

  • n 日报（复数）；电影样片；

    The film crew reviewed the dailies after shooting.
    摄制组在拍摄后审阅了样片.

2. daily routine

  • phr n 日常安排，每日常规；

    My daily routine starts with a cup of coffee.
    我的日常安排从一杯咖啡开始.

3. only

  • adv 仅仅，只有；
  • adj 唯一的；
  • conj 但是（表轻微转折）；

    She only eats vegetables for lunch. He is her only brother. I would join, only I have prior commitments.
    她午餐只吃蔬菜. 他是她唯一的兄弟. 我想加入，只是之前已有安排.

4. break

  • v 打破；中断；
  • n 休息；破裂；

    Don’t break the glass vase. Let’s take a short break after work.
    别打碎玻璃花瓶. 我们下班后稍作休息.

breakable

  • adj 可打破的，易碎的；

    Handle the breakable items with care.
    小心处理易碎物品.

unbreakable

  • adj 不可打破的，不可战胜的；

    Her unbreakable spirit inspired everyone.
    她不可战胜的精神鼓舞了所有人.

breaker

  • n 破坏者；断路器；

    The electrical breaker tripped during the storm.
    暴风雨期间电路断路器跳闸了.

5. Finnish

  • adj 芬兰的；芬兰语的；
  • n 芬兰语；芬兰人；

    She has Finnish ancestry. He speaks Finnish fluently.
    她有芬兰血统. 他能流利地说芬兰语.

Finland

  • n 芬兰；

    She visited Finland last summer.
    她去年夏天去了芬兰.

6. finish

  • v 完成，结束；
  • n 完成；终点；

    Please finish your homework before dinner. The finish line is just ahead.
    请在晚饭前完成作业. 终点线就在前方.

finisher

  • n 完成者，决赛选手；

    She was the first finisher in the marathon.
    她是马拉松比赛的第一个完成者.

finishing

  • n 完成；修饰；

    The finishing touches made the painting perfect.
    最后的修饰让这幅画变得完美.

7. hockey

  • n 曲棍球（草地）；

    He plays hockey in the local league every weekend.
    他每周末都在本地联赛中打曲棍球.

field hockey

  • phr n 陆地曲棍球；

    Field hockey is played on grass fields and is popular in Europe.
    陆地曲棍球在草地上进行，在欧洲很流行.

ice hockey

  • phr n 冰球；

    Ice hockey requires ice skates and is widely played in North America.
    冰球需要冰鞋，在北美广泛开展.s
